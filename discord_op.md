# Wintersun x SkyrimNet Integration v2.0.0

**Pray harder. Your deity is listening.**

SkyrimNet integration for **Wintersun – Faiths of Skyrim** that narrates your character's religious life and lets you commune with your deity during prayer.

## Features

**Prayer narration** — When you kneel to pray, nearby NPCs hear a narration referencing your deity:
> *{player} kneels and closes their eyes, lips moving in silent prayer to Stendarr.*

**Shrine worship narration** — When you interact with a Wintersun shrine:
> *{player} approaches the shrine and bows their head in reverence to Talos.*

**Character bio** — Your SkyrimNet character profile shows your chosen deity and rank (Follower or Devotee):
> {player} worships Stendarr. He is a Devotee, having earned full divine favour.

**Deity virtual NPC** — When you pray as a Devotee, your deity becomes a conversation partner for the duration of the prayer. Speak with them directly. Works for all 52 Wintersun deities. Voice defaults to Male Soldier — change it in the SkyrimNet WebUI.

## Installation

Install with a mod manager or extract into `Data/`. ESL-flagged — won't consume a load order slot.

## Requirements

- [Wintersun – Faiths of Skyrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22506)
- SkyrimNet (with custom triggers support)
- SKSE
- [PO3 Papyrus Extender](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22854)

## Notes

- The deity NPC only activates during prayer and only for Devotees (full favour)
- Voice can be changed via the SkyrimNet WebUI — the change persists across sessions
