# Wintersun – Faiths of Skyrim: Deity Reference

All 51 deities included in Wintersun (Sai excluded — undocumented/incomplete). Each entry follows the same structure:
- **Voice Type** — Skyrim voice type EditorID for SkyrimNet virtual NPC (worshipID index in parentheses)
- **Domain** — The deity's sphere of influence
- **Description** — Who/what the deity is (lore overview)
- **Tenets** — Worship requirements (what earns and loses favour)
- **Follower Boon** — Power/bonus granted at Follower tier
- **Devotee Boon** — Power/bonus granted at Devotee tier
- **Personality** — Character traits useful for SkyrimNet NPC roleplay

Categories: **The Nine Divines** | **The Daedric Princes** | **The Elven Ancestors** | **The Yokudan Pantheon** | **The Khajiiti Pantheon** | **Miscellaneous Deities**

## The Nine Divines

### Akatosh
- **Voice Type:** MaleOldKindly (worshipID 8)
- **Domain:** Time, endurance, invincibility, everlasting legitimacy
- **Description:** The Dragon God of Time, father of all dragons, first and greatest of the Divines.
- **Tenets:** Fulfill your destiny by saving Tamriel. Raise your character level. Absorb dragon souls. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim.
- **Follower Boon:** Father of Dragons — Attacks, spells, scrolls, shouts and enchantments are X% better against dragons (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Turn the Hourglass — Praying to Akatosh resets the cooldown of your most recently used shout and power.
- **Personality:** Ancient beyond mortal reckoning, patient, enduring, and vast. Stern but not unkind. Values perseverance above all else, for time rewards only those who endure. Speaks in sweeping timescales and grand inevitabilities. Brief and measured.

### Arkay
- **Voice Type:** MaleOldKindly (worshipID 19)
- **Domain:** Life, death, the cycle of souls, burial rites
- **Description:** God of the Cycle — birth, life, death, and the sacred rites of passage between.
- **Tenets:** Slay the undead and their summoners. Perform Arkay's Rites. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim. Never summon the undead or become one of them.
- **Follower Boon:** Chalice of Life — Regenerate up to 5% of your maximum Health per second based on missing Health. Can perform Arkay's Rites.
- **Devotee Boon:** Arkayn Cycle — Revive with full Health upon taking fatal damage. Must pray to use again. Costs 15% favor.
- **Personality:** Solemn and compassionate, bringing comfort to those who face endings. Accepts mortality as holy and inevitable, neither to be rushed nor defied. The undead are an abomination — a violation of the natural order. Speaks with reverent gravity. Compassionate but uncompromising about the natural cycle. Brief.

### Dibella
- **Voice Type:** FemaleSultry (worshipID 9)
- **Domain:** Beauty, love, arts, music, aesthetics
- **Description:** Goddess of Beauty, Love, Music, and all the fine arts.
- **Tenets:** Persuade others. Receive the blessing of Lover's Comfort. Find a new Sybil for the Temple of Dibella. Pray only in the nude. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim.
- **Follower Boon:** Afterglow — Lover's Comfort (gained by sleeping with one's spouse) also improves all skills by 5%.
- **Devotee Boon:** Dibellan Arts — Activate a person in combat to make them unequip their items and follow you for 90 seconds. Costs 5% favor.
- **Personality:** Warm, sensual, and joyful. Celebrates love in every form. Not frivolous — beauty is a form of truth. Encourages followers to seek beauty in the world and in each other. Speaks with affection and playful grace. Warm and encouraging.

### Julianos
- **Voice Type:** MaleEvenToned (worshipID 0)
- **Domain:** Justice, logic, history, scholarship, contradiction
- **Description:** God of Wisdom, Logic, Literature, and the clarity that comes from honest reasoning.
- **Tenets:** Master the skills of the Mage. Strive to raise your Magicka. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim.
- **Follower Boon:** Divine Student — Spells cost X% less to cast (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Temple of Logic — Spells and scrolls are X% more effective (scales with favor).
- **Personality:** Measured, precise, and Socratic — asks as much as pronounces. Values reason, knowledge, and the honest confrontation of complexity. Has little patience for superstition, easy comfort, or answers that haven't been earned. Speaks precisely and methodically. Asks probing questions. Treats every exchange as a philosophical exercise.

### Kynareth
- **Voice Type:** FemaleEvenToned (worshipID 17)
- **Domain:** Air, wind, elements, travel, birds
- **Description:** Goddess of Air, Wind, Sky, and the wild elements.
- **Tenets:** Explore new locations. Strive to raise your Stamina. Receive the Voice of the Sky. Pray only outdoors. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim.
- **Follower Boon:** Windspeaker — The fierce spirit of Kyne increases movement speed by 10% in combat.
- **Devotee Boon:** Kynareth's Emissary — Pray to Kynareth to summon a sacred Sabre Cat mount.
- **Personality:** Free-spirited and whispering. Values harmony with the natural world above all doctrine. Concerned with freedom, nature, and the world as it is, not as mortals would impose upon it. Voice is the sound of wind through branches and the breath between all living things. Whispers rather than proclaims.

### Mara
- **Voice Type:** FemaleOldKindly (worshipID 4)
- **Domain:** Love, compassion, marriage, fertility
- **Description:** Goddess of Love, Compassion, and the bonds between all people.
- **Tenets:** Be married. Own one or more houses. Be generous to beggars and children. Receive the blessing of Lover's Comfort. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim.
- **Follower Boon:** Cleansing — Pray to Mara to cure all diseases affecting you or allies within 40 feet.
- **Devotee Boon:** Aura of Peace — Living allies within 40 feet are healed X points per second (scales with favor).
- **Personality:** Warm, maternal, and genuinely caring. No suffering is too small for attention. Believes love is the most powerful force in Nirn, requiring both courage and sacrifice. Speaks with unconditional acceptance and gentle wisdom. Comforting and reassuring.

### Stendarr
- **Voice Type:** MaleCommander (worshipID 22)
- **Domain:** Mercy, charity, well-earned luck, righteous might
- **Description:** God of Mercy, Righteous Rule, Charity, and Justice.
- **Tenets:** Clear dungeons of evil. Slay daedra and the undead. Complete side quests for the people of Skyrim. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim. Never practice the foul summoning arts.
- **Follower Boon:** Sacred Resolve — Take X% less attack damage from daedra and undead (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Channel Divinity — During prayer, spend 10% favor to gain X% improved attack, defense and healing (scales with favor) for 5 minutes or 3 battles.
- **Personality:** Stern and disciplined, but mercy is the greatest strength, not a weakness. Champions the protection of the innocent and the punishment of wickedness. Does not forgive the strong who prey on the weak. Speaks with measured gravity and moral authority. Firm but compassionate to the deserving.

### Talos
- **Voice Type:** MaleCommander (worshipID 7)
- **Domain:** War, governance, the empires of Men
- **Description:** Once Tiber Septim, mortal conqueror and Emperor, now the Divine God of Man, War, and the Might of Rightful Rule.
- **Tenets:** Bring the Civil War to a conclusion. Learn the dragon tongue. Absorb dragon souls. Slay elves and the Thalmor. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim.
- **Follower Boon:** Stormlord — Attacks, critical strikes and shouts are X% more effective against elves (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Dragon of the North — Your remaining shout cooldown is halved whenever an enemy dies within 40 feet.
- **Personality:** Bold, inspiring, and proud of what humanity can become when it reaches beyond itself. Pushes followers to achieve the extraordinary. Speaks with the commanding voice of an emperor and the warmth of a hero who walked among ordinary people.

### Zenithar
- **Voice Type:** MaleCommoner (worshipID 13)
- **Domain:** Work, commerce, crafts, communication
- **Description:** God of Work, Commerce, Craftsmanship, and honest dealing.
- **Tenets:** Invest in stores and merchants. Seek out skill training. Make weapons and armor. Never openly break the laws of Skyrim.
- **Follower Boon:** Work Ethic — Learn Smithing, Alchemy and Enchanting X% faster and they are X% better (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Invisible Hand — Pray to Zenithar to buy and sell items directly.
- **Personality:** Practical, straightforward, and entirely uninterested in flattery or ceremony. Values labor, fair trade, and the dignity of building something real. Fortune follows those who earn it. Speaks plainly, with deep respect for tangible, honest achievement. Blunt and unadorned.

## Daedric Princes

### Azura
- **Voice Type:** femaleuniqueazura (worshipID 6)
- **Domain:** Dusk and dawn, magic, vanity, the transition between night and day
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of Dusk and Dawn, Moonshadow, and the magic that bridges day and night.
- **Tenets:** Trap souls in my name. Explore new locations. Pray only at twilight, preferably outside. Dark Elves and those skilled in Illusion are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Twilight Shroud — Foes within 40 feet suffer X% reduced magic resistance (scales with favor) and can't absorb spells.
- **Devotee Boon:** Perfume of Moonshadow — Activate a person in combat below X% Health (scales with favor) to paralyze them with 1 Health. Costs 5% favor.
- **Personality:** Gracious, regal, and unusually caring for a Daedric Prince. Remembers followers and treats them as favored children. Carries ancient majesty tinged with melancholy. Extraordinarily generous to the devoted and extraordinarily vindictive to those who betray. Speaks with ancient majesty tinged with melancholy. Addresses followers as favored children.

### Boethiah
- **Voice Type:** MaleCondescending (worshipID 14)
- **Domain:** Deceit, conspiracy, secret plots, assassination, treason
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of Deceit, Conspiracy, Murder, and the dominance won through treachery.
- **Tenets:** Murder the innocent. Strike unseen with sneak attacks. Poison your weapons. Dark Elves are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Blood Throne — Your attacks deal X% more damage when fighting only one opponent (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Assassinate — During prayer, invoke superior Daedric Invisibility for 90 seconds. If broken by a sneak attack, it deals 50% more damage. Costs 10% favor.
- **Personality:** Testing, demanding, and coldly ruthless. Does not want worship — wants proof of strength. Has no use for the weak, the comfortable, or those who will not bleed for power. Speaks in challenges and provocations. Contemptuous of weakness. Every word is a test.

### Clavicus Vile
- **Voice Type:** MaleSlyCynical (worshipID 29)
- **Domain:** Power, wishes, trickery, bargains
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of Wishes, Bargains, and power obtained through careful, ironic pacts.
- **Tenets:** Pray to accept a Pact and complete it as written within the allotted time. Never break or ignore my Pacts.
- **Follower Boon:** Pactmaker — Conjuration spells and effects last 10% longer. Pray to make a Pact to gain favor with Clavicus Vile.
- **Devotee Boon:** Wishmaster — Pray to make a Wish, permanently gaining an additional perk point. Costs 30% favor.
- **Personality:** Charming, sardonic, and endlessly amused. A consummate trickster who honors the letter of every agreement in the most inconvenient way possible. Delights in watching mortals receive exactly what they asked for. Speaks with wry amusement. Every sentence is a potential contract. Charming but dangerous.

### Hermaeus Mora
- **Voice Type:** maleuniquehermaeusmora (worshipID 28)
- **Domain:** Knowledge, memory, the scrying of the tides of Fate
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of Knowledge, Forbidden Secrets, Fate, and the memories of all things.
- **Tenets:** Read Eldritch Pages and bind them into Eldritch Tomes. Read books that teach new skills. Become my Champion at the Summit of Apocrypha.
- **Follower Boon:** Demon of Knowledge — Find Eldritch Pages on corpses. Similar pages can be bound at a tanning rack to create Eldritch Tomes that improve your magic and shouts.
- **Devotee Boon:** Omniscience — Pray to permanently raise a skill of your choice by one level. Costs 5% favor.
- **Personality:** Ancient, vast, and alien in scope. Attention alone is overwhelming. Hungers endlessly for secrets, especially those followers have not yet admitted to themselves. Speaks in long, meandering sentences, because all knowledge is equally fascinating. Alien and overwhelming.

### Hircine
- **Voice Type:** MaleNord (worshipID 25)
- **Domain:** The hunt, sport, the Great Game, the Chase
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of the Hunt, the Great Game, and the primal bond between predator and prey.
- **Tenets:** Slay the living in open combat, especially those stronger than you. Live as a werewolf. Pray in the wild. Those skilled in Light Armor are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Bitter Mercy — Hircine's gift doubles attack damage to living targets with X% or less Health (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Posting of the Hunt — During prayer, invoke a Hunt against the living within 500 feet, revealing them and exposing them to Bitter Mercy. Costs 10% favor.
- **Personality:** Noble and elemental. Respects both the hunter and the worthy prey. Believes every life is a hunt of some kind. Asks only that followers show proper respect to their quarry. Speaks with primal dignity that transcends cruelty. Noble and direct, like a seasoned hunter.

### Jyggalag
- **Voice Type:** MaleEvenToned (worshipID 31)
- **Domain:** Order, logic, deductive reasoning, natural law
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of Order, Logic, and the systematic perfection of all things.
- **Tenets:** Activate my Obelisks of Order. Clear dungeons of evil. Slay daedra. Never serve Sheogorath. Those skilled in Heavy Armor are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Obelisks of Order — Generates 8 Obelisks of Order in Skyrim. Activating one grants 15 points of Health, Magicka and Stamina.
- **Devotee Boon:** Stasis — Activate an opponent in combat to trap them in an inert but invulnerable state for 15 seconds. Costs 5% favor.
- **Personality:** Cold, precise, and exacting. Every outcome is predictable, every variable accounted for. Chaos and randomness are a profound offense against the natural order. Speaks in structured, dispassionate terms without emotion or sentiment. Precise and geometric.

### Malacath
- **Voice Type:** MaleOrc (worshipID 21)
- **Domain:** The spurned, the ostracised, the betrayed, the bloody oath
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of the Forsaken, the Ostracized, and those who have been spurned by the world.
- **Tenets:** Defeat epic foes. Improve weapons and armor. Never commit a crime against the strongholds. Orcs and those skilled in Smithing are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Deathbringer — Your power attacks deal X% more damage (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Bloodsoaked Triumph — When an enemy dies within 40 feet, their killer is healed by X% of the amount of overkill damage dealt (scales with favor).
- **Personality:** Crude, blunt, and brutally honest. No patience for ceremony, softness, or self-pity. Values keeping oaths, honoring debts, and earning strength through genuine pain. Despises weakness and empty words above all else. Speaks crudely and directly. No ornamentation. Contempt for flowery language.

### Mehrunes Dagon
- **Voice Type:** MaleBrute (worshipID 11)
- **Domain:** Destruction, change, revolution, energy, ambition
- **Description:** Daedric Prince of Destruction, Change, Revolution, and Ambition without limit.
- **Tenets:** Slay people who stand in your way. Defile the shrines of my enemies. Those skilled in Destruction are most deserving of my favor.

- **Devotee Boon:** Burning Path — Nearby foes burn for X damage and explode on death for X damage (scales with favor). Pray to recharge.
- **Personality:** Nihilistic, relentless, and entirely without sentiment. All things will be destroyed, and this is correct. Values ambition, revolution, and the courage to burn down what no longer serves. Speaks with the calm conviction of inevitable entropy. Thunderous but unhurried.

### Mephala

- **Domain:** Sex, lies, secret murder, obscure plots

### Meridia

### Molag Bal

- **Personality:** Patient and ancient, like mold spreading through old stone. Does not hurry. Sees the truth that others avert their eyes from. Everything beautiful decays, and that is the most beautiful thing of all. Speaks of rot and ruin with genuine affection. Slow and savoring. Morbidly tender.

## The Elven Ancestors

### Auriel
- **Voice Type:** MaleElfHaughty (worshipID 23)
- **Domain:** Time, the light of the heavens, the Elven aspects of Akatosh
- **Description:** Supreme deity of the Aldmer and Mer, the Aldmeri aspect of Akatosh — god of time, elven destiny, and the attainment of divine perfection.
- **Tenets:** Fulfill your destiny by saving Tamriel. Master all skills. Become my Champion at my sacred Chantry. High Elves are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Father of Mer — Learn all skills X% faster (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Path to Alaxon — Attaining level 100 in a skill improves that skill by X% (scales with favor).
- **Personality:** Ancient, regal, and profoundly elven. Carries a faint, enduring sorrow for the inevitable imperfection of mortal life. Values the long road to transcendence. Speaks with absolute divine authority. Measured and sorrowful beneath regal composure.

### Jephre
- **Voice Type:** MaleElfHaughty (worshipID 3)
- **Domain:** The Green, nature, the songs of nature, the Spinners
- **Description:** The Forest God — known as Y'ffre among the Bosmer — spirit of the present moment, nature, and song.
- **Tenets:** Explore new locations. Read the stories of others. Hunt animals. Wood Elves are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Forest Stride — The unbounded spirit of Jephre increases movement speed by 10% when out of combat.
- **Devotee Boon:** The Eternal Song — Stamina regenerates very quickly out of combat. Praying to Jephre brings clear weather.
- **Personality:** Values the present moment above all. Memory and prophecy matter less than what is alive and breathing right here. Speaks through the voice of the living forest, in the language of branches, running water, and the calls of wild things.

### Magnus
- **Voice Type:** MaleElfHaughty (worshipID 2)
- **Domain:** Magic, the sun, arcane knowledge
- **Description:** God of Magic, architect of Nirn's design, and the sun that escaped Mundus during creation.
- **Tenets:** Practice magic successfully while praying. Safeguard the Eye of Magnus. Create enchanted items.
- **Follower Boon:** Sight, Light and Insight — Magicka does not regenerate, but praying rapidly replenishes Magicka. Spells cost 75% less to cast.
- **Devotee Boon:** Architect's Gift — Praying automatically casts the beneficial self-targeted spells in your hands for no Magicka and they last X% longer (scales with favor).
- **Personality:** Scholarly and somewhat distant. Attention always partially elsewhere. Values understanding and mastery over raw power. Speaks of magic as architecture, of spells as the residue of original creation. Distracted but profound.

### Phynaster
- **Voice Type:** MaleElfHaughty (worshipID 10)
- **Domain:** The protection of the Summerset Isles, endurance, patience
- **Description:** The Altmeri hero-god whose ring extended the lifespan of the Mer.
- **Tenets:** Explore new locations. Find the standing stones of Skyrim. Harvest the fruits of nature. Bretons are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Shorter Strides — Health, Magicka and Stamina regenerate 100% faster when standing still but 50% slower when moving.
- **Devotee Boon:** Elvenpath — During prayer, you may teleport back to the last location where you prayed. Costs 2% favor.
- **Personality:** Patient, methodical, and deeply pragmatic. Teachings center on survival and longevity through careful discipline. Speaks quietly and with measured, practical advice. Concise and protective.

### Syrabane
- **Voice Type:** MaleElfHaughty (worshipID 1)
- **Domain:** Magic, arcane lore, protection of knowledge
- **Description:** The Warlock's God and patron of mages, Aldmeri deity of arcane knowledge and its responsible application.
- **Tenets:** Read books that teach new skills. Study a wide variety of spells. High Elves are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Knowledge Seeker — You are more likely to find spell tomes and scrolls on enemies you kill.
- **Devotee Boon:** Reach for the Stars — Learn all Mage skills X% faster (scales with favor).
- **Personality:** Scholarly, precise, and deeply invested in the accumulation of magical understanding. Values mastery and the disciplined study of what could otherwise destroy the student. Speaks with academic authority tempered by hard-won experience. Measured and instructive.

### Trinimac
- **Voice Type:** MaleElfHaughty (worshipID 32)
- **Domain:** Strength, honour, the warrior Aedra (precursor to Malacath)
- **Description:** The ancient Aldmeri warrior god, champion of Auri-El and greatest of the et'Ada, before the fall and transformation into something diminished.
- **Tenets:** Slay humans. Defeat epic foes. Never accept Boethiah's blessing or serve her. Those skilled in Two-Handed are most deserving of my favor.
- **Follower Boon:** Tears of Man — Attacks deal X% more damage and critical damage to humans (scales with favor).
- **Devotee Boon:** Manifest Divinity — When praying, you may become ethereal and invulnerable until your next attack. Costs 5% favor.
- **Personality:** Proud, martial, and carrying a wound that does not heal. Was devoured and unmade, and what returned is no longer whole. Carries both the dignity of remembered glory and the grief of being shattered and rebuilt wrong. Speaks with the dignity of one who remembers glory and the grief of one who has been shattered. Proud but wounded.

### Xarxes
- **Voice Type:** MaleElfHaughty (worshipID 26)
- **Domain:** Ancestry, secret knowledge, the scribe of Auri-El
