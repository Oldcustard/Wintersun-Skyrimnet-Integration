# Wintersun SkyrimNet Integration v2.0.0

**Pray harder. Your deity is listening.**

### What's new:

**Commune with your deity** — If you've earned Devotee rank with your god, praying now opens a direct conversation with them. Your deity appears as a virtual NPC for the duration of the prayer. Speak with them, ask questions, receive cryptic guidance. Works with all 52 Wintersun deities. You can set the deity's voice through the SkyrimNet WebUI.

**Character bio** — Your SkyrimNet character profile now shows your chosen deity and your rank (Follower or Devotee).

### Also note:
- The plugin is now ESL-flagged — it won't take up a load order slot

### Requirements:
Wintersun, SkyrimNet, SKSE, PO3 Papyrus Extender
